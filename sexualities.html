Sexual 
Identities

On this page you will learn about some common sexualities and what they are. 

This is the flag that stands for gay men. Gay men are men who are attracted to men.

This is a lesbian flag. A lesbian is a woman who is attracted to other women.

This is the bisexual flag. Bisexuality is when someone is attracted to thier own gender along with atleast one other gender.

This is the asexual flag. A person who is asexual doesn't expirience sexual attraction. 

This the aromantic flag. Someone who is aromantic doesn't expirience romantic attraction.

This is the polyamorous flag. Someone who is polyamorous expiriences love or has an intimate relationship with more than one person together. 

This is the pansexual flag. Pansexuality overlaps bisexuality, but they are both different identities. Pansexuality is someone regardless of gender.

This is the omnisexual flag. Omnisexuality is very similar to pan and bi, and they all overlap. Omnisexuality is the attraction to all genders.

This is a queer flag. Someone who is queer can be any part of the LGBTQ+ community. Some people choose to identify with queer because they don't want a label or are unsure of what thier sexuality is.
